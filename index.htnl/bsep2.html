Sacred Decor

The Annaprassana setup features a vibrant traditional backdrop decorated with fresh mango leaves and marigold flowers symbolizing prosperity. A comfortable seating arrangement is provided for the parents and baby, along with traditional brass lamps to create a divine and festive atmosphere.

The Feeding Ritual

We arrange a decorated feeding plate set that includes traditional bowls used during the ritual. The setup ensures the baby’s first feeding moment is celebrated beautifully with blessings from family members and elders.

Vedic Homam

An experienced Prohithar performs the Agni Homam and Ayush Homam to pray for the baby’s health, prosperity, and long life. All pooja essentials such as ghee, sacred items, fruits, and offerings are provided.

Photography & Memories

A professional photographer captures every special moment of the baby’s first feeding ceremony including the rituals, candid emotions, and family portraits so that these memories can be cherished forever.
